The Best Restaurants in Portland, Oregon

Favorite Eats in Portland.

  • Grassa

    • Portland's Pasta

    I always make a point to bring my girlfriend here whenever I’m in town. My go-to is the Sunday Pork Ragu, but honestly, you can’t go wrong with anything on the menu. Don’t skip the garlic bread—it’s a must. The atmosphere is warm and inviting, and you can even watch them hand-pull the pasta right in front of you.

    Location(s):
    1375 SE Hawthorne Blvd, Portland, OR 97214
    1506 NW 23rd Ave, Portland, OR 97210
    1205 SW Washington St, Portland, OR 97205

  • Kenny's Noodle House

    • Comforting Classic

    I've been eating at Kenny's for as long as I can remember—Those early, chilly Saturday mornings filled with the faint ambient hums of Cantonese gossip in the background followed by the comforting warmth of juk and wonton mein in my stomach to start the day.

    Location(s):
    8305 SE Powell Blvd, Portland, OR 97266

  • Fire on the Mountain

    • Wildfire Wings

    In all my time in Seattle, nothing has come close to topping these wings. They’re served fresh and piping hot, drenched in the sauce of your choice, yet still hold onto their perfectly crispy exterior. You can go classic with something like buffalo, or get adventurous with bold flavors like raspberry habanero or spicy peanut.

    Location(s):
    1708 E Burnside St, Portland, OR 97214
    4225 N Interstate Ave, Portland, OR 97217
    3443 NE 57th Ave, Portland, OR 97213
